Someone has my private photo and is blackmailing me — what do I do?

Do not pay. Block the sender. Take screenshots. Call 1930 immediately. This is sextortion and it is a crime in India.

I got a fake police call on WhatsApp saying I will be arrested

This is called Digital Arrest fraud. Hang up immediately. Real CBI, police, TRAI, ED never call on WhatsApp and demand money. Call 1930 to report.

I lost money in an online investment scam in India

Call your bank immediately to freeze the transfer. Call 1930. File complaint at cybercrime.gov.in. Do not pay any recovery fee.

Cyber crime helpline India — 1930

The National Cyber Crime Helpline number is 1930. It is free, available 24 hours, 7 days a week, all across India.
